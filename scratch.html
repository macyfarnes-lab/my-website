🌴 Saipan

Saipan is one of the most beautiful places I have ever traveled to, with clear blue water and the prettiest scenery I’ve ever seen. The island feels peaceful and not too crowded, which makes it even more special. The people there are amazing and so kind, and it honestly felt like I was in a dream the whole time.
